So on the final note, welcome.

Trading is a sceince. And I would argue the purest form of sceince. Chemistry seeks to explain chemical reactions and emergent properties thereof, then stops there. Biology rests on giants shoulders and tries to not only to explain the chemistry of DNA, but its evolution. Psychology then leans on biology to explain phenomenology in its field. Each stands on the shoulders of the more basal sceinces to explain something greater. Trading does that and more. It seeks to describe the world. In some instances, the psychology of the market, in others the biology of specific strain of wheat, and in others the chemestry of new antibiotics. Trading rests on the shoulders of giants, but seeks to do something that all sceince aspires to; trading seeks to explain the world.
